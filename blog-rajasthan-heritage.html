Palaces & Sands: Must-Visit Heritage Hotels in Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the Land of Kings, offers a travel experience unlike any other in the world: the opportunity to live exactly like royalty. Across the desert state, ancient forts, hunting lodges, and sprawling palaces have been meticulously converted into some of the world's most luxurious heritage hotels.

What is a Heritage Hotel?

A heritage hotel in India is a residence that was built prior to 1950, usually belonging to royal families or nobility, that has been restored and opened to guests. They offer modern 5-star amenities while preserving original frescoes, antique furniture, and architectural grandeur.

Top Experiences

  • Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur: A vision in white marble floating in the middle of Lake Pichola. Arriving here by private boat is unforgettable.
  • Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jodhpur: One of the world's largest private residences, part of which is managed by Taj Hotels. It offers sweeping views of the Blue City.
  • Rambagh Palace, Jaipur: The former residence of the Maharaja of Jaipur, featuring peacocks roaming the manicured gardens and unparalleled regal service.
