6-Day Golden Triangle Tour India Itinerary (Luxury Guide)

The Golden Triangle is the quintessential introduction to India. Connecting the bustling capital of Delhi, the romantic city of Agra, and the royal city of Jaipur, this route offers a masterclass in India's rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture.

Why is it called the Golden Triangle?

Looking at a map, the locations of New Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur form an almost perfect equilateral triangle. The "Golden" refers to the extraordinary wealth of cultural and historical splendor found within these three cities.

The Perfect 6-Day Itinerary Structure

  • Days 1-2: Delhi. Start by exploring the contrast between Old Delhi's chaotic charm and New Delhi's wide, leafy boulevards. Don't miss the Red Fort and Humayun's Tomb.
  • Day 3: Agra. Travel to Agra. Witnessing the Taj Mahal at sunrise is a non-negotiable experience. Later, explore the imposing Agra Fort.
  • Days 4-5: Jaipur. The Pink City awaits. Spend your time marveling at the Amer Fort, the City Palace, and the intricate facade of the Hawa Mahal.
  • Day 6: Return. Drive back to Delhi for your onward journey.

Doing it in Luxury

While the Golden Triangle is popular, experiencing it in luxury elevates the trip entirely. Think private English-speaking chauffeurs in climate-controlled vehicles, bypassing long lines with expert local guides, and retreating to 5-star heritage hotels like the Oberoi Amarvilas or the Rambagh Palace at the end of the day.
