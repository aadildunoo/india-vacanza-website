India Travel Guide

Visa Requirements & Entry

Most foreign nationals require a valid visa (except Nepal/Bhutan). India offers an e-Visa system for many countries. Apply online prior to arrival. Ensure your passport has at least six months’ validity.

Arrival Instructions

If you have a pre-arranged transfer, look for a India Vacanza sign with your name outside the Customs Hall. The area can be crowded, so look carefully!

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive Travel Insurance prior to your trip, covering accidents, medical expenses, baggage loss, and cancellations.

Baggage & Clothing

Domestic flight allowance is typically 15 kgs for Economy. Comfortable cotton clothes are best for the climate. Laundry services are widely available.

Food & Water

Savor regional cuisine! Drinking local tap water is NOT recommended; use bottled water even for brushing teeth. Bottled water is cheap and readily available.

Tipping Etiquette

Tipping inspires great service. A general guide is $4-5 daily for your driver and a similar amount for your local guides.

Electricity

Operates on 220 volts. Rounded two-or-three-pronged plugs are most common. Adapters are widely available.

Communication

Wi-Fi is common in hotels. Local SIM cards/eSIMs are easily available at airports for affordable 4G/5G connectivity. Roaming charges can be high.

Travel Within India

Indian Railways

One of the largest networks in the world. Authentic travel classes include:

  • AC First Class (1A): Private lockable cabins (2 or 4 berths).
  • AC 2-Tier (2A): Comfortable open-plan berths with curtains.
  • AC 3-Tier (3A): Economical and clean air-conditioned travel.
  • Executive Chair Class: Premium seating on Shatabdi/Vande Bharat.

Food is typically included on premium trains. We recommend booking in advance as popular routes sell out quickly.

Inquire for Rail Bookings

Domestic Air Travel

Efficient for long distances. Major carriers include:

  • Air India: Flag carrier with extensive network.
  • IndiGo: Punctual and wide coverage.
  • SpiceJet & AirAsia: Popular low-cost options.

Arrive 90 minutes early. Baggage allowance varies (usually 15kg for domestic). Well-equipped airports across major and regional cities.
